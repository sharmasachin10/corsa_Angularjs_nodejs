About Corsa

Where local car owners give travelers the perfect vehicle for their next adventure

Founded in 2009 and headquartered in San Francisco, Corsa is a car rental marketplace where travelers can rent any car they want, wherever they want it, from a vibrant community of local car owners. Travelers choose from a totally unique selection of nearby cars, while car owners earn extra money and help fuel their adventures.

A pioneer of the sharing economy and travel industry, Corsa is a safe, supportive community where the car you rent is part of a story, not a fleet.
