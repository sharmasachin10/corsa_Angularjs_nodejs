Will this void my current insurance policy?
Short answer, No. Your insurance should not be affected. We have worked very closely with large carries to help facilitate this. When you drive your car, you are covered under your existing policy. However, when a vehicle is rented through the Corsa platform, we provide a liability insurance policy to protect both you and the renter. If the renter gets into an accident, our insurance policy will go into effect, which should leave your personal policy untouched. Many policies likely have a specific exclusion for coverage when you rent out your car, which is precisely why Corsa has provided a full liability and physical insurance policy for you. This is provided at NO additional cost to you or the owner. It is because we include this at no cost, a renter may not opt to waive coverage.

Who sets the insured value?
In the event of a total loss, you will receive every cent of your stated insured value. No depreciation. No messing around. No hassle. Every vehicles insured value is set by the owner and agreed to prior to any rental. Values are based on an agreed upon figure with the help of the Hagerty® Value Guide and top industry market value tools. Once Corsa and the owner of the vehicle agree to a set value, only then will the vehicle be available to be rented out on the platform.
